Hi, I'm Shreyansh. I'm an undergrad studying Electrical Engineering at IIT Roorkee, though my true focus is on artificial intelligence. I love tinkering with machine learning systems, computer vision, and natural language processing to build things that actually matter — whether that's diagnosing diseases from medical scans or automating complex data analysis.

Recently, I worked as a Machine Learning Engineer Intern at Infigro.ai, where I built an AI agent to autonomously interpret charts and visual data. Prior to that, as a Research Intern at IIT Roorkee's EE Department, I explored the intersection of Control Systems and Cybersecurity by simulating network attacks.

Outside of my research and coursework, I competed at the InterIIT Tech Meet, where my team secured 4th place. You'll usually find me building open-source projects or collaborating with the Data Science Group on campus.
